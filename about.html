Cryptonet Consulting Services

Cryptonet Consulting was conceived in 2018, with Aaron Lebel, founder of Cryptonet Technologies LLC, taking on a myriad of consulting roles over the course of his education at Carnegie Mellon University. His first role was programming an Asset Market simulation for the Tepper School of Business, and he has since worked with numerous companies including Corner LLC, Carnegie Mellon University, and the Information Networking Institute (INI). Aaron has been working as an independent consultant for two years, and has helped companies and startups grow by assisting with web development, back-end design, and blockchain integration.
